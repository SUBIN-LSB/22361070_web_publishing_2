VERY BIG NEWS

Breaking news. An unbelievable incident has occurred at a Napoli Family Restaurant that has been bustling since its grand opening.

Whether it’s because no one believes it or due to someone’s conspiracy, I, reporter Lee, have worked tirelessly to get permission

to publish this incredible big news in the newspaper. After about 22 failed attempts, I finally got the chance to write about this incident.

The more you try to find this pasta restaurant, the harder it is to locate, and when you least expect it, it suddenly appears.

The unbelievable, or rather, hard-to-believe big news is that a total of five people have gone missing from this restaurant.

These five missing individuals were all from one family: two siblings and three pet cats. We are currently distributing flyers about them.

If you see these flyers or if anyone has spotted them, please do not tell anyone and instead contact me directly.

Remember, this is the best course of action for everyone involved.

- WHERE ARE THEY -

Where could they all have gone? If you have any information, please contact me.

The first missing person is an adult male, the oldest among them. He has short hair and wears braces, and he has a younger sister.

The second missing person is an adult woman, the second oldest among them. She has long, light-colored hair and appears tired.

The third missing individual is their first cat. It is the second oldest among all the cats, chubby, lazy, and enjoys eating.

The fourth missing individual is their second cat. It is the oldest among all the cats and has poor teeth, making it difficult for it to eat.

The last, fifth missing individual is their youngest cat, and among all the cats, it is the youngest. It resembles a tiger in appearance and is active.
